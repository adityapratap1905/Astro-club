About HEAW-2025

IUCAA Centre for Astronomy Research and Development (ICARD), GLA University, Mathura is organizing HEAW-2025 during November 18-20, 2025. The purpose of the workshop is to provide opportunities to UG/PG students and Research Scholars to gain exposure to current issues in High Energy Astrophysics and its implications in other research areas.

Topics to be Covered

  • Introduction to the Stars and Black Holes
  • Current issues in Compact Object Astrophysics
  • Computational issues in X-ray and Gamma-ray Astrophysics
  • Physics of Relativistic Jets and Blazars

Workshop Coordinators

  • IUCAA, Pune: Dr. Vaidehi Paliya
  • GLA University, Mathura: Dr. Ashutosh Singh
  • GLA University, Mathura: Dr. Dinesh Chandra Maurya
